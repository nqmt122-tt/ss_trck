The Logistics "Blind Spot"

Legacy Inefficiency

  • Lagging Data: Historical stats are weeks or months old.
  • Informal Channels: Real-time demand lives in Facebook Groups, Zalo Chats, and TikTok.
  • Market Volatility: Surge pricing (+30% costs) occurs because signals are missed.

"We are optimizing based on the past, while the market lives in the present."

The Consequence

Shippers

Panic booking and surge pricing premiums.

Carriers

Inefficient routes and costly empty return legs.

Phase 3: The Desperation Index (SSM)

Kalman Filtering

Social data is inherently noisy. We apply State-Space Models (SSM) to filter out the chatter and find the true market state.

  • Noise Cancellation: One person spamming "URGENT" isn't a crisis. 100 people is.
